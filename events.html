Bravest of the brave: The Indian Army

NCC IIT Roorkee brings to you the first webinar of a series of events, on the topic ‘Bravest of the Brave: The Indian Army’. Our speaker for the event, a very eminent personality, is Lt. Gen. Satish Dua, PVSM, UYSM, SM, VSM(R), and during the webinar, he will talk about his experiences in the Army during 4 decades of his service. The webinar will be an opportunity for all those enthusiastic about the Indian Army to know more about the IA, and life in the IA, and motivate those who are willing to don the uniform.

Date & Time - 07-02-2021

Venue - Online
